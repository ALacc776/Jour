### Description
Capture your daily moments instantly.

DayLog is a simple journaling app built for speed and privacy. You can log your day in seconds using our quick entry format or just write freely. It is completely up to you.

Key Features:

Lightning Fast Entry
- Quick entry mode lets you treat one line as one entry
- The text field is always visible so you can just type
- No complex forms or setup required

Visualize Your Life
- Track your consistency with beautiful heatmaps
- Browse your memories in the calendar view
- Search through all your entries instantly

100% Private and Offline
- No accounts, cloud sync, or tracking
- Everything stays securely on your device
- Works completely offline

Rich Memories
- Attach photos from your camera or library
- Use time based prompts for Morning, Afternoon, and Night
- Set daily reminders to help build the habit

Easy Export
- Copy your entries to the clipboard in a list format
- Perfect for backing up to Notes or Google Docs

DayLog feels as simple as the Notes app but it is designed specifically for daily logging. There are no overwhelming features, just a clean space for your thoughts.

Download DayLog today and start your journey.
