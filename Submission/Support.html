Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?

Yes! All your journal entries are stored only on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers.

How do I back up my entries?

Your entries are automatically included in your iCloud backup if you have it enabled in iOS Settings. You can also use the 'Export Data' feature to create a manual backup.

Can I export my journal?

Yes! Go to Settings → Export Data to save your entries as a text file. You can also use the 'Copy' features to copy entries to your clipboard.

How do I set up daily reminders?

Go to Settings → Enable Notifications, then choose your preferred reminder time.

What if I accidentally delete an entry?

Unfortunately, deleted entries cannot be recovered. We recommend using the Export feature regularly to back up important entries.
