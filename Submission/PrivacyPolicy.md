# Privacy Policy for DayLog
*Last updated: December 2025*

> **Quick Summary:** DayLog stores your journal entries locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data to our servers. Your privacy is our priority.

## 1. Information We Collect
DayLog is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:
* **Journal Entries:** Your journal entries are stored locally on your device only
* **Usage Data:** Basic app usage statistics (streak counts, entry dates) stored locally
* **No Personal Information:** We do not collect names, email addresses, or other personal identifiers

## 2. How We Use Your Information
Your journal entries and data are used solely for:
* Displaying your entries within the app
* Calculating your journaling streak
* Providing app functionality (search, filtering, calendar view)
* Local backup and restore features

## 3. Data Storage and Security
Your data security is our top priority:
* **Local Storage:** All data is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage
* **Encryption:** Journal entries are encrypted using iOS Keychain services
* **No Cloud Sync:** We do not sync your data to any external servers
* **No Third-Party Access:** Your data is not shared with any third parties

## 4. Data Sharing
We do not share your personal information with anyone:
* No data is transmitted to our servers
* No data is shared with third-party services
* No analytics or tracking services are used
* Your journal entries remain private to you

## 5. Your Rights
You have complete control over your data:
* **Access:** View all your entries within the app
* **Export:** Export your entries as text or JSON files
* **Delete:** Delete individual entries or all data
* **Backup:** Create local backups of your data

## 6. Data Retention
Your data is retained as long as you choose:
* Data remains on your device until you delete it
* No automatic deletion of your entries
* You can delete all data at any time through app settings

## 7. Children's Privacy
DayLog is appropriate for all ages:
* No personal information is collected from anyone
* Safe for children to use with parental supervision
* No social features or external communication

## 8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Changes will be:
* Notified through app updates
* Posted with updated "Last updated" date
* Maintained with the same privacy-first approach

## 9. Contact Us
If you have questions about this privacy policy:
* Email: support.and@proton.me
* Support: Available through app settings

---
> **Your Privacy Matters:** DayLog is built with privacy by design. Your thoughts, memories, and personal reflections stay with you and you alone.
